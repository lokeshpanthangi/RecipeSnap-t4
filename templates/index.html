About RecipeSnap

RecipeSnap is an AI-powered cooking assistant that helps you make delicious meals with what you already have in your fridge. Our application uses advanced image recognition and natural language processing to identify ingredients and generate creative recipe recommendations.

Our technology stack includes:

  • Image Captioning: nlpconnect/vit-gpt2-image-captioning
  • Object Detection: facebook/detr-resnet-50
  • Recipe Generation: mistralai/Mistral-7B-Instruct

No more food waste or wondering what to cook! RecipeSnap turns your available ingredients into culinary inspiration.
